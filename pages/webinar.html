WEBINAR SERIES

The SIGGEN webinar series is proposed as a strategy to promote knowledge on various NLG topics and strengthen SIGGEN membersâ€™ collaboration.
We welcome proposals on any topic of interest for the community (including faculty, students and industry) and at any level (introductory, intermediate or advanced). You are invited to submit a proposal to share your experience and expertise, present ongoing work or introduce industry projects that could be interesting for the community. Each webinar will have a running time of approximately 60 minutes, including about 30-40 minutes for presentation, with the remaining time spent on questions and discussion.

Proposals must be submitted to siggenboard@googlegroups.com, including the following information:
  • Webinar title and short abstract (about a paragraph)
  • Presenter name and affiliation
  • Target audience (faculty, students, industry,...)
  • Webinar level (introductory, intermediate, advanced)
  • Received proposals will be reviewed by the SIGGEN board, scheduled in communication with the speakers, and advertised well in advance.

If you have an idea for a webinar, but are not sure if it could be interesting for the community, just ask! Send us an email and we will discuss your idea together.
